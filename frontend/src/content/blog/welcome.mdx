More often than not, we find ourselves wondering if we're heading in the right direction whether it's in our studies, careers, relationships or family responsibilities. We constantly question our capabilities and whether we've made the right decisions when things don't go as planned. Well, that's perfectly normal because we're all human, after all.

## The Mirror Question

Ever wanted an answer to the question above? Go back to the mirror, look into the reflection of your own eyes, and ask yourself, "Are you the real one who you think you are?" In that moment, your mind may starts to point out your flaws more than the things you've accomplished and the good you've done for your friends, family and society.

Based on my experience, I feel that finding yourself doesn't end at a particular age, nor it is a destination to reach.

It's all about seeing yourself in the things you do, finding purpose, and adding value to the lives of others. You naturally attract people who share your energy, and expecting nothing in return for the good you do is a remarkable quality everyone should cultivate. Because whenever you act selflessly, you open yourself to learning more and becoming willing to take risks. Eventually those risks, more often than not, lead to growth and help you evolve into a better version of yourself than you were before.

## The Path Defines You

It's usual to have self doubt, obsession over something and being busy chasing our dreams. But the process and the path you choose to make your progress reaching there will define you and reflect who you actually are! People like you and me usually come and go, but if we make efforts being a remarkable time stamp in others calendar, collating these time stamps, that perspective, those kind words will embrace your presence with them in your absence.

## Life is a Rainbow

Life is nothing short of a rainbow, full of colours. We usually see just a thin white light and it's boring, right? If you wanna see its true side, your good deeds should shine on others life droplet, where the prism with seven colours would showcase the one you actually are!
